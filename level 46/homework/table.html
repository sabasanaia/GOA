საზომი ერთეული ინფორმაცია
rem Relative to font-size of the root element
% Relative to the parent element
px Pixels (px) are relative to the viewing device. For low-dpi devices, 1px is
one device pixel (dot) of the display. For printers and high resolution screens 1px implies multiple device pixels.
vh Relative to 1% of the height of the viewport
